Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Social Media app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information beyond what you provide during registration.

Account creation is handled directly through Firebase without third-party verification.

Reporting Content

Users can report inappropriate content via the "report" feature.

If content is marked with isReport = true, we will review it and may remove it if it violates policies against violence, harassment, or offensive behavior.

Data Usage

Your data is used only to provide and improve the app's core functionality.

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
